Who We Are

Crafted for Aspiring
Bartenders

Haldwani Bartending Academy Academy was founded with a singular vision — to bring world-class bartending education to the hills of Uttarakhand. Located in Haldwani, we train students for careers in five-star hotels, luxury resorts, and bars across India and abroad.

  • Hands-on training with real bar setups and equipment
  • Industry-certified instructors with 10+ years experience
  • Partnerships with leading hospitality brands in India
  • 100% practical curriculum — not just theory
  • Placement assistance across Uttarakhand, Delhi NCR & beyond
  • Small batch sizes — personalized attention guaranteed
